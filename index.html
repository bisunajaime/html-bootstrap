Research Topic


The aim of this research is to provide the community with a renewable and clean source of energy in terms of biomass.

As of the moment, the community uses LPG or Liquid Petroleum Gas and charcoal; however, these aren't very cost efficient and environmentally friendly. With the use of banana leaves, a prominent waste factor in the community, this will be converted into a source of fuel which is cost-efficient and environmentally friendly in order to replace the current sources of fuel used.

Reflection

Research is such a tedious subject. At first, choosing a topic was very difficult knowing that are so much ways to help the community of Sitio Makabuhay, but we ended up choosing the aspect of energy knowing that one of their main problems is electricity, so we sought for a way to provide a renewable source of energy in terms of biomass. Secondly, creating this research paper took a team's effort to successfully produce this outcome. Lastly, the defence was a success. With the mistakes and problems considered, we were able to deliver the results and recommendations to our panelists. In the end, we are hoping that this research will be approved to fulfil our main goal which is to help the community of Sitio Makabuhay.
